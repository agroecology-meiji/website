About Agroecology Lab

Turning ecological knowledge
into agricultural insight

Field Experiment DNA / Omics Data science

We view agricultural fields not only as places for crop production, but also as ecosystems where diverse organisms interact. Building on ecological and data-analysis approaches developed through mycorrhizal and microbial community research, we study relationships among crops, soil, and microbes.

Our focus is not only on which organisms are present, but also on when, where, and how they function in agroecosystems.
