Department of Agriculture, Meiji University

Agricultural fields are not only places for growing crops.

Plants, fungi, microbes, insects, soil, and the surrounding environment interact and change over time.

The Agroecology Lab studies these hidden relationships and applies ecological knowledge to sustainable agriculture.

We observe, cultivate, measure, and analyze. Through fieldwork and experiments, we explore how living organisms support agricultural systems.
