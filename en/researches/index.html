Research

We study relationships among plants, fungi, microbes, and insects to understand and apply sustainable agroecosystems.

Topics

Research themes

Microbiome

Environmental change and microbiomes

We investigate how microbial communities in agricultural fields and plant rhizospheres change under different environmental conditions.

  • Environmental DNA and amplicon sequencing
  • Community structure and environmental drivers
  • Time-series analysis
Mycorrhiza

Mycorrhiza and plant stress tolerance

We focus on symbiotic relationships between plant roots and fungi to understand mechanisms supporting plant growth and stress responses.

  • Mycorrhizal fungal community analysis
  • Plant–microbe interactions
  • Evaluation of stress responses
Agroecology

Microbial diversity and resilience

We explore how biological networks contribute to the stability and resilience of agroecosystems.

  • Species interactions
  • Diversity and stability
  • Visualization of agroecosystems
Data-driven

Microbiomes and biosensing

We use data analysis and prediction methods to infer environmental states from microbiome and omics data.

  • R / Python analysis
  • Multivariate analysis and prediction models
  • Research data visualization
